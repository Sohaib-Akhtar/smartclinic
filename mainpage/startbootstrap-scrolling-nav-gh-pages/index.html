About Us

Smart Clinic System is a web based service which enables the patients and doctors to connect before meeting in reality :

  • Patients can view details of the doctor and make an appointment from home and share their experiences online
  • Doctors can build their own reputation and strengthen relationships between their patients and interact with other doctors as well
  • Provides a membership based system to all its users.
